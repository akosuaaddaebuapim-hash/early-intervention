About Stella

Who Stella is

A developmental intervention specialist with over ten years of experience as an educator. Stella supports families with practical therapy and coaching.

Philosophy

Early intervention works best when caregivers are partners. We focus on play, routines, and small strategies that fit daily life.

Mission

To help babies and young children with developmental delays thrive by giving families the tools and ongoing support they need.

Services

Practical supports for children families and schools delivered with care.

Therapy Services

Individual therapy plans focused on communication play and daily routines for infants and preschool children.

Educational Toys and Resources

Curated toys and activity guides that support developmental milestones and playful learning.

Support for Families and Schools

Workshops in class coaching and strategy planning for educators and caregivers.
