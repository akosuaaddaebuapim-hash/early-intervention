About Stella

Who Stella is

A developmental intervention specialist with over ten years experience as an educator. Stella partners with families to create learning moments in everyday life.

Philosophy

Intervention is most effective when it is family led playful and embedded in routines. We set meaningful goals and coach caregivers to sustain progress.

Mission

Help children with developmental delays thrive and give families the tools and confidence they need to support lifelong learning.

Services

Specialist supports for infants and young children families and early years settings delivered with care and practical guidance.

Therapy Services

Individual plans focused on communication play and daily routines for young children.

Educational resources

Curated toys guides and activity sets designed to support milestones through play.

Support for families and schools

Workshops coaching and in class support to embed strategies across settings.
