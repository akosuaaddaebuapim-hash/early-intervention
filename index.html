About Stella

Who is Stella

Stella Amoako is a developmental intervention specialist and educator with over ten years of experience. She works closely with families to create everyday learning moments that build skills and confidence.

Philosophy

Early intervention works best when caregivers are partners. Stella uses playful routines and practical strategies that families can use every day.

Mission

To help babies and young children with developmental delays thrive by giving them and their families tools and support.

Services

Practical supports for children, families and schools delivered with warmth and expertise.

Therapy Services

Individual therapy plans for babies and young children with developmental needs. Sessions focus on communication, play and daily routines.

Who: families with infants and preschool children

Educational Toys and Resources

Carefully chosen toys and activity guides that support milestones and meaningful play.

Support for Families and Schools

Workshops, coaching and in class support to help caregivers and teachers carry strategies into daily life.

Starting early improves communication, play and daily living skills. Families learn sustainable strategies that last.
